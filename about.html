ABOUT THE ARTIST

Kim Prentice is the artist behind Glass N' Baubles. She started working with stained glass in 2021 and quickly grew to love it, leading her to create Glass N' Baubles shortly after.

Kim is originally from Manitoba, Canada, but moved to North Carolina in 1997. When she's not crafting hand-made stained glass pieces, Kim enjoys reading as well as spending time with her family and friends.
